In the world of music, talent often runs in the family.

Adonis Graham Drake’s son, the offspring of the globally renowned rapper Drake, has recently stepped into the limelight with the release of his debut music and a captivating music video for “My Man Freestyle.”

Drake is one of the most successful and influential rappers in the world, but he is also a proud father to his son, Adonis Graham.

Adonis, who turned six years old on October 11, 2023, surprised everyone with his debut song and music video, “My Man Freestyle”.

The song showcases Adonis’ rap skills and personality, as well as his bond with his father and his love for basketball.

In this blog, we will explore who Adonis Graham is, how Drake confirmed his fatherhood, what “My Man Freestyle” is about, how Drake supported his son’s musical venture, and what are the reactions to Adonis’ song.

Who is Adonis Graham?

Adonis Graham is the son of Drake and Sophie Brussaux, a former adult film star and artist.

He was born on October 11, 2017, in France.

His full name is Adonis Mahbed Graham, which means “lord” or “ruler” in Greek.

He has blue eyes and curly blonde hair, which he inherited from his mother’s side of the family.

Drake and Brussaux met in Amsterdam in January 2017, shortly after Drake’s split from Jennifer Lopez.

They had a brief fling, which resulted in Brussaux’s pregnancy.

However, Drake initially denied that he was the father of Adonis, and even accused Brussaux of lying and extorting him.

Brussaux claimed that she had proof that Drake was the father, and hired a lawyer to seek child support from him.

How did Drake confirm his fatherhood?

Drake confirmed that he was the father of Adonis in June 2018, when he released his fifth studio album, Scorpion.

On the album, Drake addressed his son and his relationship with Brussaux on several songs, such as:

“Emotionless”, “8 Out of 10”, and “March 14”.

On the latter track, Drake revealed that he only met his son once at Christmas time, and that he was trying to co-parent with Brussaux despite their differences.

He also expressed his regret for hiding his son from the world, and his hope for a better future for him.

Drake also shared photos of his son for the first time on Instagram in March 2020, along with a heartfelt caption about missing his family and friends amid the COVID-19 pandemic.

He wrote: “I love and miss my beautiful family and friends and I can’t wait for the joyful day when we are all able to reunite.”

Since then, Drake has posted more pictures and videos of Adonis on social media, showing their close and loving relationship.

What is “My Man Freestyle”?

“My Man Freestyle” is Adonis’ debut song, which he released on his sixth birthday, October 11, 2023.

The song is produced by Boi-1da, one of Drake’s longtime collaborators, and features Adonis rapping over a catchy beat.

The lyrics are mostly in English, but also include some French words and phrases, reflecting Adonis’ bilingual background.

The song is about Adonis’ confidence and swagger, as well as his admiration for his father and his passion for basketball.

Some of the notable lines from the song are:

  • “I’m Adonis Graham, I’m the man / I got blue eyes like my maman / I got curly hair like my dad / I got big dreams like my granddad”
  • “I love my daddy, he’s the best / He always gives me hugs and kisses / He always makes me laugh and smile / He always teaches me new things”
  • “I love basketball, it’s my sport / I play like Kawhi Leonard / I shoot like Steph Curry / I dunk like LeBron James”
  • “I’m a star in the making / I’m a legend in the waiting / I’m a king in the rising / I’m a rap god in the training”

The song is available on all streaming platforms, such as Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, etc.

How did Drake support his son’s musical venture?

Drake supported his son’s musical venture by appearing in the music video for “My Man Freestyle”, which was released on October 12, 2023.

The music video is directed by Director X, another one of Drake’s frequent collaborators, and features Adonis rapping in various locations, such as a recording studio, a basketball court, a playground, and a mansion.

Drake joins Adonis in some of the scenes, showing their father-son chemistry and having fun together.

The music video also features a cameo from Toronto Raptors rookie Gradey Dick, who is Adonis’ godfather and Drake’s close friend.

Dick plays basketball with Adonis and Drake, and gives Adonis a Raptors jersey with his name and number on it.

Drake is a well-known fan and ambassador of the Raptors, and has often brought Adonis to their games.

The music video has over 100 million views on YouTube, and has received positive reviews from critics and fans alike.
