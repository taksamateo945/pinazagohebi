It has been nearly a decade since Vince Vaughn has been able keep the wedding vows with his real estate agent wife. And for the family, the actor is a proud father of two children with his wife.

Vince Vaughn is an American actor, producer, screenwriter, and comedian, who has been appearing in the movies and TV shows since the late 1980s. He is best known for his role from the 2006 comedy movie, Wedding Crashers from which he won the number of awards.

Vince Vaughn Bio; Parents, Education, Height

Vincent Anthony Vaughn was born on 28 March 1970 in Minneapolis, Minnesota. His parents are Vernon Lindsay Vaughn, a former salesman for a toy company and Sharon Eileen, a real estate agent, and a stockbroker. He has two sisters, Valeri, and Victoria Vaughn.

Standing at a towering height of 6 feet 5 inches (1.96 meters), Vaughn attended Lake Forest High School. Since a young age, he had a passion for acting and used to attend musical theater.

His Career; Movies, TV Shows, Net Worth

Vince began his acting career from the 1989 season of TV series              China Beach. His debut movies were 1993 biographical Rudy, where he played the role of Jamie O’Hare. Moreover, his movies role include Domestic Disturbance (2001), Couples Retreat (2009), Delivery Man (2013), Term Life (2016), and Fighting with My Family (2019).

Besides, Vaughn is a familiar face on the number of TV shows. Some of his television credits include CBS Schoolbreak Special (1990), The Larry Sanders Show (1998), Going to California (2013), Saturday Night (2013), and Live and True Detective (2015).

Married To Real Estate Agent Wife; Children

The 49-year-old is currently relishing his married life with wife Kyla Weber. Kyla, aged 40 is a Canadian real estate agent. The couple got hitched on 2nd January 2010 after their 2009 Valentine’s Day engagement.
