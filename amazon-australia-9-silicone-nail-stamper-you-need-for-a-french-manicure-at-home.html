Chloe Morello said the ‘genius’ trick for getting coloured tips at home works perfectly for short nails and is ‘so easy compared to freehand or using nail tape’.

To try Chloe’s hack for yourself, all you need is a base coat clear nail polish, the colour of your choice for the tip, some acetone, a lip brush and a top coat of nail varnish.

You can also add sparkle or glitter if you want to make your manicure look even more professional.

‘How genius is this nail hack? I saw it somewhere and knew I needed to try it for myself,’ Chloe posted on Instagram.

‘To try it for yourself, paint a little bit of base coat over the surface of your nail, and then paint some colour onto the top of your finger in a straight line.

‘After this, press it carefully onto the top of your nail so that you get a little French tip.’

Following this, Chloe said you should coat the entire surface of your nail with a top coat.

Then, you can ‘jazz’ up your manicure by adding gold foil or glitter over the top of an extra swipe of top coat.

Finish with a final top coat, taking care to go over your tip for ‘extra longevity’ and healthy nails. 

You can also tidy up any mistakes using acetone on a lip brush.

How to do a French tip manicure at home 

Chloe shared the simple six-step method on her Instagram profile (pictured)

1. Paint base coat over your nails one by one.

2. Paint some colour onto the top of your index finger in a straight line and press it carefully onto the top of a nail on your opposite hand so you get a little French tip.

3. Go through your nails one by one doing this and then apply a top coat to every single nail.

4. Swipe a little extra top coat over half of each nail and gently add gold foil or glitter.

5. Seal with a final top coat, taking care to go over your tips for ‘extra longevity’ and healthy nails.

6. You can tidy any mistakes using acetone on a lip brush.
