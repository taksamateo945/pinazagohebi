Brian McKnight Biography

Brian McKnight is best known as an R&B singer. R&B singer-songwriter broke through in 1997 with his album Anytime. Among other honours, he was nominated for sixteen Grammy Awards. He was born on 5 June 1969 in Buffalo, NY. At the 2014 ESPY Awards he performed “Side Parts” alongside Drake. His high school, Sweet Home High School, he was the bandleader.
