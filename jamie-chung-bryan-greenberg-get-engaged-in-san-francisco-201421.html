Jamie Chung and Bryan Greenberg Get Engaged in San Francisco!

 Posted on June 7, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  157 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

'Tis the season! Actors Jamie Chung and Bryan Greenberg are officially engaged after almost two years of dating, a source confirms to Us Weekly. (E! News was first to break the story.) Greenberg proposed in San Francisco over the holidays. 

On Dec. 27, Chung, 30, tweeted, "So lovely being back home. I [heart] SF," and Greenberg tweeted several photos from the popular California destination over Christmas. 

But a few days later the couple was off to ring in the New Year in the Turks and Caicos Islands.
