Who is Craig Ferguson?

Craig Ferguson calls both Scotland and the US home. The guy was born in Glasgow way back in 1962 before eventually making his way stateside when he moved to New York City in 1983.

Always a bit of a comedian and entertainer at heart, Craig found his biggest success hosting The Late Late Show on CBS from 2005 to 2014.

His unique interview style was on full display when he sat down with Archbishop Desmond Tutu in 2009; no wonder their chat earned a prestigious Peabody Award.

Between his Scottish roots and all the top-notch TV work he took on, there’s no doubt Craig Ferguson belongs among the most accomplished imports in the entertainment industry.

How old is Craig Ferguson?

Craig Ferguson is 61 years old as of 2023.

He was born on May 17, 1962, making him a Taurus in the zodiac.

Craig Ferguson’s career

Craig Ferguson started his career in the UK in music and comedy.

He drummed for a punk band and performed comedy under the name Bing Hitler. He also acted on stage in shows like The Rocky Horror Show.

Ferguson moved to the US in 1995, landing his big break on The Drew Carey Show.

He went on to write and star in a few of his movies, too.

Additionally, he appeared in films such as Lemony Snicket and The Muppets.

In 2005, Craig took over as host of The Late Late Show, putting his unique spin on the late-night format over 8 years and 2000+ episodes.

He showcased his many talents through segments like Geoff Peterson and Secretariat.

Not one to rest on his laurels, Craig later hosted game shows like Celebrity Name Game, winning two Daytime Emmys.

He’s also hosted history shows and produced a web series with his wife.

On the animated side, he’s lent his voice to the How to Train Your Dragon franchise, among others.

Along with all that, Craig has written books chronicling his life and career journey.

All in all, he’s led a full and creatively flourishing professional path from the start in the UK to today.

Craig Ferguson’s Personal Life

Craig Ferguson has been quite open about the personal struggles he’s faced over the years, including alcoholism and mental health issues.

In 1992 he got sober after a drinking binge that nearly took his life.

Since then, he’s been a strong advocate for addiction recovery and preventing suicide.

Marriage-wise, Craig has walked down the aisle three times. His first ended in divorce in the 1980s, and his second in the early 2000s. Both unions resulted in a son for Craig.

Happily, his third marriage has gone the distance since 2008 – he and his wife have another son together.

Born in Scotland, Craig holds dual citizenship in both the UK and the US after achieving a perfect score on his naturalization test in 2008.

He remains fiercely proud of his Scottish heritage too, often incorporating his cultural traditions into public appearances.

All in all, Craig Ferguson has lived quite the full and fascinating life story despite overcoming personal demons along the way.

Does Craig Ferguson have a wife?

Yes, Craig Ferguson has a wife. He is married to Megan Wallace Cunningham, an art dealer from Vermont.

They tied the knot in a private ceremony on her family’s farm in 2008.

Both have a son named Liam, who was born in 2011.

They also have a web show together called Couple Thinkers, where they explore various topics with experts and celebrities.
