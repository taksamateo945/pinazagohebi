Lindsay Price, Curtis Stone Married: Actress, Celeb Chef Wed in Spain

 Posted on March 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  223 words  |  Martina Birk

Another June wedding! Actress Lindsay Price and celeb chef Curtis Stone got married in Spain on Saturday, June 8, Us Weekly can confirm.

The couple tied the knot on the island of Mallorca at the Hilton Sa Torre Mallorca Resort in Spain, where guests, including Price’s Beverly Hills, 90210 costar, Tiffani Thiessen, marveled at the hotel’s beautiful and historic architecture, which dates back as far as 1369. Thiessen posted a picture from the reception of she and her husband, actor Brady Smith, to her Instagram page, writing, “Toast to our dear friends for an amazing wedding weekend in Mallorca, Spain. #memories #unforgettable #stonewedding.”

Price, 36, and Top Chef Masters host, Stone, 37, got engaged in July 2012 after three years of dating and welcomed a son, Hudson, in November 2012.
