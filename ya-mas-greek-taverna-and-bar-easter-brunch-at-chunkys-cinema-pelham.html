Join Ya Mas Greek Taverna & Bar For Our Grand Easter Brunch Buffet hosted at Chunky's Cinema. 

Easter Bunny will be at brunch giving treats to all kids and will have plenty of opportunities for pictures.

After purchasing your tickets please Call Ya Mas Greek Taverna @ 603-635-4230 so that you may choose your time slot for your reservation. Each reservation will have a 2-hour time slot to enjoy their holiday!
