No makeup needed for this natural beauty! Jenna Dewan Tatum hopped on Instagram on Wednesday, Aug. 26, to sing the praises of her latest butt-kicking workout—and showcase her gorgeous face without makeup.

Using Beyonce‘s “Crazy in Love” for caption inspiration, the Witches of East End alum, 34, wrote, “Your love got me looking so crazy right now @shapewithangela BEST butt kicking inspirational workout as always!”

While the shout-out to celeb trainer Angela Manuel-Davis was sweet, the true focus of the photo was Mrs. Channing Tatum‘s flawless complexion, sans a stitch of foundation, mascara, or lipstick. She rounded out her look with tousled tresses, a black tank, and golden necklace.

The au naturel look is not too shocking on the actress, though! Dewan Tatum has confessed that since becoming a mom to 2-year-old Everly, her beauty routine has become pretty simplified. “I love Josie Maran Argan Oil. I put it on my face, hair, and body. It’s moisturizing and gives you a good glow,” she told Birchbox.com in April 2014. “I also never leave home without Aquaphor. It’s the best lip balm!”
