Toe-rmented Deacon Gives Ridge An Earful, Accuses Him Of Kidnapping Sheila?

 Posted on April 19, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  504 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The Bold and The Beautiful spoilers and updates tease a tormented Deacon Sharpe (Sean Kanan) will give Ridge Forrester (Thorsten Kaye) an earful about Sheila Carter (Kimberlin Brown).

No one seems to believe Deacon saw ten toes on the body going in the cremation oven, might Deacon accuse Ridge of kidnapping Sheila and doing something with her?

The Bold And The Beautiful Spoilers: Deacon Sharpe Is Tormented

Deacon is tormented over what he saw but no one believes his story-even the crematorium operator’s not vouching for the ten toes on the corpse!

Everyone who he’s told has pretty much thought Deacon’s lost it, although Ridge’s opinion is Deacon lost it when he fell for Sheila.

Deacon’s upset because if that wasn’t Sheila that went in the oven and who Steffy Forrester (Jacqueline MacInnes Wood) stabbed to death, then Sheila must be somewhere!

Deacon will never believe that Sheila would have set up a doppelganger to go to the cliff house while she ran off somewhere else.

He will never believe that his “mama” just dumped him because of the difficulty of his family accepting their love either-so where is she?

B & B Spoilers: Ridge Forrester And Bill Spencer Trapped Sheila Carter

It wasn’t lost on Deacon how sneaky Ridge and Bill Spencer (Don Diamont) were when they set up an FBI sting to take down Sheila, Bill fake-romancing her.

They even forced Deacon to go along with it when she was calling him, not knowing her phone was bugged-for all he knows, Ridge or Bill kidnapped the real Sheila!

In the meantime, Deacon didn’t realize that Deputy Chief Bradley Baker, (Dan Martin) now Deputy Director, has given Steffy a copy of the death certificate and a statement from the crematorium.

When Deacon tells his tale to Ridge a confrontational argument ensues and Ridge tells him the body was Sheila, no question-and that Deacon’s lost his mind.

The Bold And The Beautiful Spoilers: Deacon Sharpe Lashes Out, Ridge Forrester Gives Him A Reality Check

Deacon lashes out and wants to know what Ridge and Bill or both of them did with the real Sheila, or did they pay the morgue to burn someone else’s body?

Was this a setup to put Sheila away in prison somewhere and was the doppelganger their setup too, to frame her but it went sideways when Steffy stabbed her?

Deacon is in devastating and tormenting grief the way it is and everyone thinks he’s crazy for real; how is he to have closure when the corpse wasn’t Sheila?

Ridge gives him a reality check-they have proof the body that went into the morgue was Sheila’s, and Deacon must have hallucinated the corpse had ten toes!

Then the thought occurs to Deacon-what if this was the doppelganger’s setup and she kidnapped or killed the real Sheila; Deacon has to find her!
