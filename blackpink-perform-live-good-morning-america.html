BLACKPINK Will Perform Live on 'Good Morning America' Next Week

 Posted on April 17, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  233 words  |  Larita Shotwell

BLACKPINK is set to make their American morning show debut on Good Morning America next week! The girls are confirmed for a live studio performance on Tuesday, February 12th in Times Square.

The news comes after the GMA social media account tweeted hints about the group’s potential appearance on the show. Yesterday, the account tweeted black and pink hearts along with the message, “We have a big announcement coming tomorrow morning.” Fans immediately went into a frenzy and thought the tweet was about BLACKPINK.

Last week, GMA producer Tony Morrison also tweeted about the girl group, asking fans to tell him everything they know about the four-piece.

BLACKPINK is currently one of the hottest K-pop acts right now. Their international fanbase is massive, which has been proven with their record-breaking music video for “DDU-DU-DDU-DU.” The video accumulated 36.2 million views in its first 24 hours, the most for any Korean artist and only second overall.
