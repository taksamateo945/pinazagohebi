Early life

The successful businessman was born on October 10, 1984, in the heart of Domboshava in a family of four. His mother and one brother recently died. He is believed to have started embracing small business transactions at a young age of 17 years. He then embraced the art of providing gas for domestic use, where he worked as a middle-man.

Career

He secured his first contract through a friend who was working at the Angolan Airways offices. He started focusing on gas sales to various users, which saw him making a fortune with time. Within a year of business, he purchased a car, and in two years, he purchased a Mercedes Benz S Class. He later established Pioneer Gases, which today is part of the Piko Trading Group. The company provides gas to the industrial, commercial, retail, and public sectors.

He then purchased Quick Gases in Botswana, where he is currently the only shareholder. He is also linked to Rivonia Gases and City Centre Freight. These companies fall under the PIKO Trading Group, where he is a majority shareholder.
