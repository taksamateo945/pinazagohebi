Keira Knightley Gives Birth, Welcomes First Child With James Righton

 Posted on May 26, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  186 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Ready for (knight)time stories!

Keira Knightley and her musician husband James Righton have welcomed their first child together, multiple sources confirm to Us Weekly. The couple are the proud new parents of a baby girl.

Us confirmed in December 2014 that the Everest actress was expecting. She rarely spoke about the pregnancy, but did show off her growing baby bump in several haute couture gowns while promoting The Imitation Game during awards season. Back in February, she particularly turned heads in a floral Valentino dress at the 87th Annual Academy Awards.

That same month, Knightley, 30, joked about going into labor during an appearance on The Ellen DeGeneres Show.
