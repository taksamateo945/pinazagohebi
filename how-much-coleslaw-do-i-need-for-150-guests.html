How many servings is a pound of coleslaw?

The average coleslaw serving size is about 3 ounces, or around 1/3 cup. If you’re buying pre-shredded cabbage, about 140 ounces of cabbage is appropriate. If you’re starting with whole heads of cabbage, estimate that 1 pound of cabbage will feed about five people

How far in advance can coleslaw be made?

Coleslaw is easy to make in advance. The vegetables can be shredded a day or two in advance and kept in a resealable bag in the refrigerator.
